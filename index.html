About Us

Near Twiga is a forward-thinking software development company that is committed to tackling the major challenges facing our world today.

Our focus is on developing innovative software solutions that address issues such as climate change, human-wildlife conflict, and hunger. We believe that technology has the power to make a real difference in the lives of people and the planet.

we specialize in blockchain-based solutions, utilizing the latest software development technologies to deliver cutting-edge products that are secure, transparent, and efficient. From blockchain-based supply chain management systems to decentralized applications for social impact, we are dedicated to creating solutions that drive positive change. Join us in our mission to create a better future for all

Our featured solutions

Discover our top software solutions in the featured products

Tenebo

A web app, "Tenebo", allows users to adopt wildlife animals as NFTs and use the proceeds to build shelters for affected communities. Adopters receive a unique digital certificate and regular updates on their adopted animal and the shelter projects. Our platform helps to solve human-wildlife conflict and support conservation and community development efforts

Visit
Hambre

"Hambre", aims to tackle hunger by connecting farmers with the resources they need to succeed. Through the platform, farmers can access financing and equipment, while users can rent idle land and purchase farm inputs. By providing these resources and connections, we hope to empower farmers to increase food production and alleviate hunger in their communities
